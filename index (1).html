Our Mission

India faces a growing issue of stray dogs being removed from communities, often without proper adoption or care. Pawfect Match focuses on rehoming these strays while helping anyone adopt cats or dogs safely. Our goal is to connect humans with pets, reduce stray suffering, and promote responsible adoption practices.

Did You Know?

  • Over 30 million stray dogs live across India.
  • Many stray dogs face inhumane removal methods instead of adoption.
  • Adopting strays can reduce disease spread and improve community safety.
  • Strays are loving and resilient, perfect companions for caring homes.
